welcome to Merchant's FieldApp
These are the major steps you should follow, if you want to use this app effectively as a Merchant
1. Create wallet for each new customer, using the Create thrif wallet menu, and fill out the form
2. Proceed to Thrifts Set Up to assign thrift value to the wallets you created
3. Furthermore use the payments link to keep record of all deposits and withdrawals
4. Anytime you can check a customer's account balance
5. The links under VAS helps you activate E-mail , SMS , Notification and UI/UX for customes who need them
These Procedures might be of help as an admin
1. Use the remittals menu to keep records of cash recieved from merchants
2. The approval menu helps you credit the wallet of customers who made payments to merchants
3. The Payments link helps you keep records of all monies paid to customers through their merchants
